Netflix TV Show/Movies Analysis

This project analyzes Netflix’s global catalogue using Tableau to uncover insights on content distribution, ratings, genres, geography, and release trends. Using a Kaggle dataset, I prepared, modelled, and visualized over 6,000 titles to understand Netflix’s strategic focus across Movies and TV Shows. Findings revealed a strong bias toward Movies (68%) over TV Shows (32%), heavy adult-oriented content (TV-MA), and genre strengths in Documentaries and Comedy. Regression-style trend visuals showed rapid content expansion from 2014–2019, then a decline post-COVID. The analysis recommends increasing international TV shows, diversifying into family and animated genres, investing in regional productions across Africa and Asia, and optimizing content formats with shorter films and mini-series to drive engagement, retention, and sustainable global growth.
