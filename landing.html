Sales Channel Analysis

This Power BI project connects to ContosoRetailDW (SQL Server) via DirectQuery for live analysis, with Power Query ETL and star-schema modelling, and DAX KPIs (Sales, Gross Margin, Profit %, YoY, AOV). Interactive dashboards compare Store, Online, Reseller, and Catalog performance and surface regional trends to reveal where revenue and margin are truly coming from. The insights guide marketing allocation, inventory planning, and channel-mi optimisation, helping leaders prioritise the highest-ROI products, locations, and campaigns. Deployed to Power BI Service, it delivers secure, self-service access and dependable refresh for executive decision-making.

Hola Cookies Company

This project uses a Microsoft Access relational database I designed for Hola Cookies Group as the governed data source, with Power BI Desktop handling Power Query ETL, star-schema modelling, DAX KPIs, and Row-Level Security, then publishing to Power BI Service for secure stakeholder access. The dashboards analyse customer behaviour, product performance, and store profitability, revealing customer demographics, seasonal sales trends, product return rates, and gross profit margins. Leaders can pinpoint high-ROI SKUs and stores, target promotions to the right segments, and optimise inventory and staffing by season. In short, it’s an end-to-end BI solution that turns operational data into clear, actionable decisions.

Waggle Pet Device Comparitive Analysis

At Waggle, I built a boardroom-ready Power BI report to assess the viability of Lapcat, the feline counterpart to the successful Lapdog device. Using Power Query ETL, star-schema modelling, and DAX KPIs in Power BI Desktop—then publishing to Power BI Service for secure executive access—I analysed data from 1,000 Lapcat prototypes and benchmarked results against Lapdog. The dashboards tell a clear comparative story across adoption, engagement, reliability/returns, and revenue-margin projections by customer segment. The outcome equips leadership with evidence-based go/no-go guidance on product launch timing, marketing focus, and investment priorities—ensuring decisions are driven by data, not intuition.

Automated Sales Report Process & Forecasting

This project automates the end-to-end weekly sales workflow using Power Automate, SharePoint, OneDrive, Power BI, and Excel. New files dropped into OneDrive are validated, versioned, and routed to SharePoint; historical data is archived and appended to a curated dataset for trend analysis. Power Query handles ETL, DAX powers KPIs and forecasting, and Power BI Service delivers near real-time dashboards for revenue, margin, and variance tracking. The solution eliminates manual handling and reporting inconsistency, reduces errors and turnaround time, and strengthens data protection through centralised governance. Leaders now have trusted, always-current insights for performance tracking and accurate weekly forecasts—freeing teams from ad-hoc tasks and accelerating decision-making.

National Clothing Market Analysis

This project provides a data-driven market analysis for a national clothing chain using Power BI, SQL, and regression modelling. Customer purchases, population, and income data were integrated to reveal how income and demographics influence spending behaviour across U.S. states. Power Query managed ETL, while DAX measures powered KPIs and predictive models. Regression analysis identified strong correlations between income and purchase value (R²=0.78) and between population and total sales (R²=0.74). The insights enable precise customer segmentation—targeting low-income shoppers with budget products and high-income groups with premium collections. The solution empowers leaders to make data-informed pricing, marketing, and stock allocation decisions, replacing guesswork with predictive, evidence-based strategy for market expansion and profit optimisation..
